### Town Guard
The Town Guard is the first line of defense in a large village or town, a professional group responsible for maintaining order and protecting key areas like gates and walls.
Well-trained and better equipped than the militia, they are called upon to respond swiftly to threats both in peacetime and during larger conflicts.
- They are Tier 3 of Militia Frontline
- They are also their own separate tier with a certain min Resource value. So a few of them will appear early while the rest must come through upgrading
- AI: PrefersToWait = true
- Stats:
	- 70 Resolve
	- 20 Melee/Ranged Defense
- Weapons:
	- Arming Sword
	- Goedendag
	- Boar Speer
	- Pike,
- Perks:
	- Vigilant
	- Poise (to have more effective armor without dropping better armor),
- Weaponspecific: Shield Expert

### Constable
The constable would be more focused on maintaining order and enforcing laws rather than leading military operations.
While their role is essential for community stability, it is generally not as prioritized in a combat-oriented scenario.

### Captain
A militia captain typically leads the local militia and has command over the troops during times of conflict.
This includes tactical planning, organizing defenses, and direct engagement in battles.
They are likely to be more focused on military operations and strategies.
