### Changes: ###

0.8.1
	- Increase **Insect Swarm** AP cost to 4 (up from 3) for **Goblin Shamans**
	- Rename **Incendiary Bags** into **Incendiary Shot**
	- Fix wrong item descriptions for incendiary bags

0.8.0
	- Add **Goblin Taskmaster** as new lower tier version of the **Goblin Overseer**. They cost 40% less resources, have -20 Stamina, +5 Melee Skill, 0 Ranged Skill, -15 Ranged Defense and -10 Initiative. They always come with a **Thorned Whip** equipped, the **Whip** skill (4AP) and the **Anticipation** perk (on top of all basic Goblin perks)
	- Rename existing **Goblin Shaman** to **Goblin Shaman Elder**
	- Add **Goblin Shaman** as new lower tier version of the **Goblin Shaman Elder**. They cost 50% less resource, have -10 Hitpoints, -10 Resolve, -30 Stamina, -10 Melee Skill and -10 Ranged Defense. They use the same perks and skills as the regular **Goblin Shaman**, except that they use a **Lesser Root** skill, which only roots a single target, instead of up to 7
	- Add **Free Lancer** as new Elite Unit for Mercenary and Brigand Parties. They cost the same as Hedge Knights, but have -10 Stamina, +10 Melee Skill, -20 Melee Defense, +40 Initiative. They are heavily armored and use either **Pike** or **Bladed Pike**. They have **Dodge**, **Fresh and Furious**, **Vigorous Assault**, **Savage Strength**, **Skirmisher**. In Base Reforged they also gain **Leverage** and **Finesse**. In Hardened they gain **Nailed It**, **Spear Mastery** and **King of all Weapons** instead
	- Add **Ironguard** as new Elite Unit for Mercenary and Brigand Parties. They cost the same as Hedge Knights, but have +10 Stamina and -20 Melee Skill. They are heavily armored and always spawn with a **Heavy Metal Shield**. They have **Battle Forged**, **Resilient**, **Shield Expert**, **Underdog**, **Exploit Opening**, **Finesse**, **Line Breaker**, **Survival Instinct** and **The Rush of Battle**. In Hardened they also gain **Warden**
	- Add **Firebrand** as new Elite Unit for Mercenary and Brigand Parties. They cost the same as Hedge Knights. They have 100 Hitpoints, 80 Resolve, 120 Stamina, 70 Melee Skill, 90 Ranged Skill, 20 Melee Defense, 15 Ranged Defense and 160 Initiative. They are lightly armored and always wield a throwing weapon and a **Battle Whip**. They have **Backstabber**, **Anticipation**, **Dodge**, **Resilient**, **Footwork**, **Offhand Training** and **Throwing Mastery**. In Base Reforged they gain **Pathfinder** and in Hardened they gain **Elusive**. They also have an unlootable **Bottomless Bag** equipped in the accessory slot, which equips randomly either an **Acid Flask**, **Fire Bomb** or **Daze Bomb** into their offhand at the end of their turn
	- Improve body armor selection of **Sellswords**
	- Add new standalone AI scripts for `throw_fire_bomb_skill`, `throw_acid_flask` and `throw_daze_bomb_skill` allowing NPCs to use them correctly
	- Remove fix for vanilla faction limit
	- Fix **Sellswords** having slightly wrong strength values
	- Fix tooltips of Caves displaying the wrong tactical tile for where the battle will be in
	- Add `location::CP_TacticalTextOverwrite = null` for assigning custom location tooltip texts to complete the line "This location is "

0.7.0

0.6.0
	- Add new **Bag of compressed Nets** and **Large Bag of compressed Nets** ammo items for Firearm: When you reload your weapon its next shot will deal 80% less damage and put every enemy hit in a net
		- The normal bag is sometimes sold by alchemists
	- Add **Bag of incendiary Bullets** and **Large Bag of incendiary Bullets** ammo items for Firearm: When you reload your weapon its next shot will convert all damage to Fire Damage and light the respective tiles on fire for 2 turns on a hit
		- The normal bag is sometimes sold by alchemists
	- Add six new recipes to craft those four new ammo items
	- Add new **Reinforced Wooden Stafff** weapon as a lower tier version of the **Polemace**. It has the same weapon type, range and attack patterns. It has 35-55 Damage, 120% Armor Damage, 40% Armor Penetration, 6 Reach and a Weight of 10
		- It is sometimes sold in southern marketplaces
		- **Nomad Cutthroats** have a 10% chance to spawn with this weapon
	- Add new cave layout for tactical combat which is used for beast and orc cave fights
	- Orc Caves and Beast Caves now hide their defender
	- Orc Caves now use the same name generation as Beast Caves
	- Add five additional prefixes for Cave Names
	- Improve food and loot item drop pool of **Witch Hut** locations. They now may drop a **Green Vial** (10%) or **Blue Vial** (10%)
	- Rename the existing southern **Citizen** to **Peasant**
	- Add new **Citizen** unit as the Tier 2 upgrade over **Peasant**. They cost 12 resources. They have the same perks and slightly better stats and gear than their Tier 1 version
	- Add new southern **Caravan Hand**. They have the same cost and stats as their northern counterpart and similar gear. They will now appear in southern caravans
	- Add new **Personal Guard** unit. They cost 25 resources. They have similar stats and skills to the caravaran guard. Their behavior is similar to that of the **Heralds Bodyguard** in that they try to stay close to their protected targets
	- Add new **Councilman** unit as the Tier 3 upgrade over **Citizen**. They cost 12 resources. They have slightly worse gear than a citizen but slightly better stats. They will always spawn together with **Personal Guards**. They have a 10% chance to appear in regular caravans and are guaranteed to spawn in the rare heavily armed caravan variant
	- Add new **Sellsword** unit as the Tier 2 upgrade over [melee] **Mercenaries**. They cost 55 resources. They have better stats, better quality gear and gain the **Poise** perk in addition to all the other perks from the **Mercenary**
	- Add new **Crowntaker** unit as the TIer 2 upgrade over [melee] **Bounty Hunter**. They cost 50 resources. They use higher quality helmets, weapons and reinforced nets. They have **Anticipation**, **Executioner**, **Small Target**, **Angler** and **Throwing Mastery** in addition to all the perks from regular **Bounty Hunter**
	- Add `location.m.CP_TacticalTypeOverwrite` and `location.m.CP_EngageImageOverwrite`to assign custom tactical types and images when fighting this location
	- Add `skill.m.CP_IsAttachedSkill = false` which can be set to true, to make this skill stay with its item, even if that is unequipped

0.5.0
	- Add new lower tier necromancer called **Hooded Man**. He has very similar stats and cost to the vanilla Necromancer, but loses Soul Link and Inspiring Presence.
	- The existing Necromancer is buffed to become the Tier 2 version of the Necromancer caster. He costs 50 (up from 30), grants 500 XP (up from 350), has 90 Hitpoints (up from 50), 200 Stamina (up from 80) and minor improvements to combat stats. He never spawns with a hat
	- Add new Hexen Location with 210 Base Resources. They spawn only in forests or towns near settlements. Up to two of them can exist at any given time
	- Hexen Parties are no longer randomly spawned into the world. They now only exist as roaming parties sent from these new locations
	- Add new **Physicians Gathering** Situation, that can randomly occur in any size 2 or size 3 settlement. It increases amount of available medicine, reduces medicine price and makes it so more Anatomists and Monks are available for hire
	- Add new **Grand Travelling Show** Situation, that can randomly occur in any size 2 or size 3 civilian settlement or in city states. It increases food price, available recruits and it so more Juggler, Houndmaster and Wildmen are available for hire
	- **Forgetfulness Potion** now refunds 2 random perks (up from 1)
	- Increase Base Resources of Lindwurm caves to 210 (up from 140)
	- Set Movement Speed of roaming Ghoul and Hyena parties to 90% (down from 100%)
	- Add three new suffixes for cave names
	- Fix missing sign in falcon item tooltip
	- Add new `CP_getApplicableRandomSituations()` function to `settlement.nut` that returns a WeightedContainer with all applicable random situations
	- Modularize onUpdate of add_random_situation_action using new CP_getApplicableRandomSituations to determine which situation can appear

0.4.0
	- Add new southern medium settlement, which always belongs to one of the southern city states, and offer a mix of southern and northern contracts
		- 3 of these spawn randomly at the start of each campaign. So this will not affect existing campaigns
	- All Caves now spawn a roaming parties every 2 days, which use half of the camps resources (scaled by day in the same way camps are scaled). They roam the near area for 1 day and then return to the camp.
		- Direwolves, Ghouls, Hyenas, Serpents and Webknechts spawned this way have a VisibilityMult of 0.5
		- Caves spawned before this update, will not spawn roaming parties
	- Caves now have 140 Resourecs (up from 120) and 0.4 VisbilityMult (down from 0.75). In addition to a guaranteed treasure, Caves now will either drop a second treasure or 5-10 tools
	- All Caves can now spawn up 5 tiles closer to settlements or other locations
	- Direwolf, Ghoul, Serpent, Hyena and Webknecht Caves can now appear up to 3 times the in world (up from 2)
	- Lindwurm, Unhold and Frost Unhold Caves can now appear up to 2 times the in world (up from 1)
	- Serpents, Lindwurm and Unhold Caves now only start spawning at day 10+
	- All Beasts, which are now covered by Caves will no longer randomly spawn in the world
	- All other Beasts (Alps, Hexen, Schrats, Kraken and Ifrit) now have a shared maximum of 8 parties (down from 15 for all beasts combined)
	- Add five new tips of the day describing unhold camps spawn behavior and caves in general
